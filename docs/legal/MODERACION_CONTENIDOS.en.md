# Content moderation statement — AkoeNet

Last updated: 2026-04-06

This document serves as a **statement of moderation practices** required under digital services rules (including Regulation (EU) 2022/2065 — DSA — where applicable to hosting intermediaries and online communication platforms). It complements the Terms of Service (/legal/terminos) and the Privacy Policy (/legal/privacidad).

## 1. Scope

AkoeNet is a community platform (servers, channels, direct messages, voice). **Users** generate content; **server owners and moderators** may apply additional rules within their community, without prejudice to platform-wide rules.

## 2. Prohibited content

Content and conduct prohibited under the **Terms of Service** include, without limitation:

- Illegal content under applicable law.
- Harassment, threats, hate, or serious discrimination.
- Content that infringes third-party rights (intellectual property, privacy, etc.).
- Spam, malware, or fraud attempts.
- Abusive evasion of blocks or moderation decisions.

Details and examples may be expanded in the Terms. Servers may set **additional rules** visible to their members.

## 3. How we moderate

- **Human moderation:** platform administrators (where applicable) and **server moderators** appointed by the server owner may delete messages, remove users from a server, or apply **bans** with a duration or permanent, using available tools.
- **Automated / technical moderation:** the backend may reject or filter certain text using profanity lists (e.g. library integration) and rate limits; this does **not** replace human review where needed.
- **User reports:** users may **report** channel and direct messages; notifications are logged for review.

## 4. Consequences of a breach

Depending on severity and repeat behaviour:

- Removal or editing of content.
- Warning or restrictions on the account or server.
- Kick or **ban** from the server.
- Account suspension or closure in serious or legal cases.

## 5. Appeals and complaints

If you believe a **platform-level** moderation decision (not only within a private server) is wrong, you can:

1. Contact the **legal / DSA** address shown in the site footer (dedicated email), describing the fact, affected content or account, and grounds for appeal.
2. Exercise your data protection rights under the Privacy Policy (/legal/privacidad) and the DPO form (/legal/dpo) where personal data processing is involved.

For disputes **within a specific server**, the first instance is usually that server’s moderation team; AkoeNet does not replace community autonomy unless content violates the law or general Terms.

## 6. Single point of contact for authorities and notices (DSA)

For **official communications from authorities**, **illegal content notices**, or other **legal** queries about the service (distinct from general product support), use the email address published in the website footer (legal / DSA contact). Do not use that mailbox for general product incidents if a separate support channel exists.

## 7. Changes

We may update this statement. The last revision date is at the top. Continued use after material changes may require you to accept the Terms again when indicated.
