# Cookie and local storage policy — AkoeNet

Last updated: 2026-04-06

This page describes the use of cookies and similar technologies (including browser storage, `localStorage` / `sessionStorage`) on the AkoeNet website and web application, in accordance with applicable rules (including the ePrivacy Directive and national laws such as Spain’s LSSI).

## 1. Controller

The controller is as stated in the Privacy Policy (page /legal/privacidad).

## 2. What cookies and local storage are

**Cookies** are small files the site may store on your device. **Local storage** lets the application save data on your device persistently or for a session so the app can function.

## 3. Classification we apply in AkoeNet

### 3.1 Strictly necessary / technical (no prior consent required)

These are essential for the service you request (sign-in, security, basic UI preferences). They include, among others:

| Key / scope | Purpose |
|-------------|---------|
| `token`, `refresh_token` | Keep you signed in securely (JWT and renewal). |
| `akoenet_session_notice` | One-off session messages (e.g. security notices). |
| `akoenet_onboarding_v1` | Remember that the welcome tutorial was shown. |
| `akoenet_ui_theme_active_uid` | Theme preference (light/dark) per user. |
| Voice / volume settings in `localStorage` tied to account or channel | Voice channel behaviour per your preferences. |
| `landing_locale` (or other landing language keys) | Remember the language chosen on the public page. |
| Cookie consent (`akoenet_cookie_consent_v2`) | Remember your choice on optional categories. |

Without these, many features (login, voice, preferences) cannot work properly.

### 3.2 Optional / non-essential (consent required when used)

The current product build does **not** load third-party analytics cookies (e.g. Google Analytics) by default. If such tools are added in the future, they will only activate if you **accept** that category in the banner or settings.

The cookie banner lets you **Accept** (include optional tools when they exist), **Reject** non-essential items (only strictly necessary storage remains), or **Configure** categories.

## 4. Managing your choice

- On your first visit you will see the **cookie banner** with Accept, Reject, and Configure.
- You may change your mind when we update categories: we will show the banner again or an equivalent control.
- Your browser can also block or delete cookies: see Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge help. Note that blocking **technical** cookies may prevent sign-in or normal use.

## 5. Retention

Technical session data are kept as long as needed for the stated purpose (e.g. until you sign out or the token expires). The cookie consent key is stored so we do not ask on every visit unless the policy changes materially.
