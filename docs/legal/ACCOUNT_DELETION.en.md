# Account deletion (AkoeNet)

## How to request deletion

You can request deletion of your AkoeNet account in **two ways**:

1. **Inside the app:** open **User settings** and use the **account deletion** / erase account option.
2. **Privacy (DPO):** contact our privacy team using the **DPO form** on the website:

   **https://akoenet.com/legal/dpo**

   (If your deployment uses a different public domain, replace the host with the correct one.)

## What we delete or anonymise

After deletion, we **delete or anonymise** personal data tied to your account, including (non‑exhaustive):

- username
- email address
- profile information (avatar, banner, bio, custom status, etc.)
- authentication credentials and sessions
- linked accounts / integrations tied to the profile, where applicable

Exact wording may evolve with the product; also read the **Privacy Policy** at `/legal/privacidad`.

## What may be retained temporarily

For **legal compliance**, **platform safety**, and **moderation**, some records may be **kept in a limited form**, for example:

- moderation logs and content reports
- records needed for abuse or fraud prevention
- information required for regulatory compliance

**Retention periods** depend on applicable law and legitimate safety needs; there is not always a single fixed duration.

## Timelines

- Requests from **User settings** follow the in‑app flow; processing time may depend on system load.
- **DPO** requests are handled according to the process published on `/legal/dpo`.
