# Child Safety Standards

AkoeNet maintains a zero-tolerance policy against child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

We strictly prohibit any content, behavior, or activity involving child sexual abuse material (CSAM), grooming, exploitation, trafficking, or any form of abuse involving minors.

Our safety standards include:

- User reporting tools for inappropriate content and abusive behavior
- Content moderation and account review processes
- Immediate removal of illegal content
- Permanent suspension of offending accounts
- Cooperation with law enforcement and relevant authorities
- Compliance with regional and international child safety regulations

Users can report child safety concerns directly through the application or by contacting our safety team.
